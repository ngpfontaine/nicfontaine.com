Background

I'm a North Florida migrant to Asheville, North Carolina for education at the University of North Carolina at Asheville, and a graduate of the comprehensive New Media program specializing in 3D Animation under the tutelage esteemed industry professionals and trusted friends Christopher Oakley and Chris McKee.

After spending some professionally diverse years in AVL, I relocated to San Francisco in the summer of 2016 - where I'm currently located, at least for a while.

'Not' Work

Creative Abstract

I have strong convictions on design and user-experience. I want to challenge conventions, push my technological base, and identify personally with every project on which I work; while growing creatively from each new experience.

In my free time I prefer to prod the potential of my capabilites applied to new and old technologies. One of my favourite themes is exploration; while giving the user enough information to engage with a product, allow them to modify and sometimes break to fit their unique needs.

I love finding the unique edge-cases that seem to only arise in release and trying to accomodate for them - and the humbling experience of knowing you, again, failed in planning for every possibility (and Jeff Goldbloum visits you in your dreams to explain chaos theory).
