Professional Background

I'm currently working as a front-end developer driving UX designs & web/Unity3d applications at Mursion, Inc.

Before SF, I was in Asheville, NC designing landing pages & display ads for PPC campaigns / running AB testing at Element-360, and designing for games, animation, and branding under various freelance contracts. I graduated from UNC Asheville in 2012, and grew up in Tallahassee, FL.

Most of my current time is spent in:

  • Linux, Node/Php, Vanilla JS, HTML, CSS - deploying responsive, lightweight, user-friendly web-apps
  • Unity3D, Photoshop, & C# - crafting logical interfaces for dashboards, in-game HUDs, and VR
  • Pen & Paper, Sketch, Invision, & more - wireframing user experience, prototypes, and designing assets

Creative Abstract

I have strong convictions on design and user-experience. I want to challenge conventions, push my technological base, and identify personally with every project on which I work; while growing creatively from each new experience.

In my free time I prefer to prod the potential of my capabilites applied to new and old technologies. One of my favourite themes is exploration; while giving the user enough information to engage with a product, allow them to modify and sometimes break to fit their unique needs.

I love finding the unique edge-cases that seem to only arise in release, and trying to accomodate for them - and the humbling experience of knowing you, again, came up short in the hypothetical storytelling of planning for every possibility. (And that night Jeff Goldbloum visits you in your dreams to seductively explain chaos theory).
