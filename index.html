Creative Abstract

I have strong convictions on design and user-experience. Coming from a background of late nights spent in powerful native applications, I prefer a direct, heavy-handed appproach to software design — that is, building out, around the most frequent actions of the user, as opposed to pulling in an opinionated front-end library as a templated project base.

I love finding the unique edge-cases that seem to only arise in release, and trying to accomodate for them - and the humbling experience of knowing you, again, came up short in the hypothetical storytelling of planning for every possibility. (And that night Jeff Goldblum visits you in your dreams to seductively explain chaos theory).
